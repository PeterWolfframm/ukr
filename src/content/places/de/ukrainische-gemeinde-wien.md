The Ukrainian Community Vienna provides essential support services, cultural events, and a welcoming space for Ukrainians in Austria. Legal advice, language courses, and integration assistance available.

## Our Mission

The Ukrainische Gemeinde Wien has been serving the Ukrainian diaspora in Vienna for over two decades. Since 2022, we have significantly expanded our services to support those fleeing the war.

### Services We Offer

#### Legal Assistance
- Asylum application support
- Residence permit guidance
- Family reunification help
- Employment law advice

#### Language Programs
- German courses (A1-C1)
- German for specific professions
- Children's language support
- Integration courses

#### Community Support
- Welcome orientation for newcomers
- Housing assistance
- Job placement help
- Psychological support referrals

### Events & Culture

We host regular cultural events to maintain connections to Ukrainian heritage:

- Monthly community gatherings
- Ukrainian holiday celebrations
- Art exhibitions
- Film screenings
- Children's activities

### How to Get Involved

Whether you need help or want to give back, we welcome you. Contact us during office hours or attend one of our open community events.
