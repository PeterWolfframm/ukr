Experience authentic Ukrainian cuisine in the heart of Kyiv. From traditional borscht to modern interpretations of varenyky, every dish tells a story of resilience and heritage.

## About HIMARS Kitchen

HIMARS Kitchen represents the best of Ukrainian culinary tradition, reimagined for the modern palate. Our chefs combine time-honored recipes passed down through generations with contemporary techniques and presentations.

### Signature Dishes

- **Borscht Supreme** - Our award-winning take on the classic, served with pampushky
- **Varenyky Selection** - Choose from potato, cherry, or cheese fillings
- **Chicken Kyiv** - The original, prepared the traditional way
- **Salo Tasting Board** - For the adventurous, with various preparations

### Atmosphere

The restaurant features a warm, contemporary space that honors Ukrainian folk art through subtle design elements. Large windows offer views of Khreshchatyk Street, perfect for people-watching while enjoying your meal.

### Reservations

We recommend booking ahead, especially for weekend dinners. Walk-ins welcome based on availability.
