Ukrainian-speaking general practitioner in Vienna's 7th district. Dr. Kovalenko offers comprehensive family medicine services with a focus on helping Ukrainian refugees navigate the Austrian healthcare system.

## About Dr. Kovalenko

Dr. Olena Kovalenko completed her medical training in Kyiv before moving to Austria, where she has been practicing for over 15 years. She is fully licensed in Austria and speaks Ukrainian, Russian, German, and English fluently.

### Services

#### General Practice
- Routine health check-ups
- Acute illness treatment
- Chronic disease management
- Preventive care

#### Specialized Support
- Health system navigation for newcomers
- Document translation assistance
- Referrals to Ukrainian-speaking specialists
- Mental health screening and referrals
